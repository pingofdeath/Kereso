Disambiguation in Wikipedia is the process of resolving the conflicts that occur when articles about two or more different topics have the same "natural" title. Most of the pages in this category are disambiguation pages containing no other content, only links to other Wikipedia pages.

Notes:

  • Do not include this category manually in articles; the {{disambig}} template automatically does this.
  • Do not add articles to these sub-categories or create additional sub-categories until you read Manual of Style (disambiguation pages)—most of the sub-categories do not comply with the MOS and will be removed.
  • Links to disambiguation pages other than from the top of articles are usually mistakes. Find and fix them at disambiguation pages with links.
