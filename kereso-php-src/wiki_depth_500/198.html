This category is used for administration of the Wikipedia project. It is not part of the encyclopedia.
It contains pages which are not articles, or lists articles by status, rather than content.

This category includes all pages marked as needing references by {{unreferenced}}. Because of its size, users may prefer to work with Category:Articles lacking sources, which subdivides reference requests based on when they were requested.

The oldest is currently Category:Articles lacking sources from June 2006.

This category exists primarily as an aid to bots and other automated processes.
