Directed_cycle.png (200 × 183 pixel, file size: 3 KB, MIME type: image/png)

A directed cycle graph, created by Derrick Coetzee, who releases all rights to it and places it in the public domain, in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. The edges go in a clockwise direction around the outside; not a simple cycle, since blue vertices are used twice.
