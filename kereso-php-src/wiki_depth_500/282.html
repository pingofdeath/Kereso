Hi. I'm a Research Professor with the Robotics Institute and the Associate Director of the National Robotics Engineering Consortium at Carnegie Mellon University. My research interests include path planning, multi-robot control, computer vision, system architecture, and artificial intelligence for construction, agricultural, mining, and exploration robots.
